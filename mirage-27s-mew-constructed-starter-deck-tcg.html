The Mirage's Mew Constructed Starter Deck (Japanese: 構築済みスターター まぼろしのミュウ Pre-built Starter Mirage's Mew) is a Japanese-exclusive Half Deck Starter Deck that complements the Mirage Forest expansion of the Pokémon Trading Card Game.

Information

The majority of these cards could not be found in the main expansion. Those that were not were added to the main set when the English version was made, excluding Plusle and Minun, which were added to the EX Trainer Kit 2 set.

Like many other Half Decks, the Mirage's Mew Constructed Starter Deck also includes a rulebook, damage counters, status counters, and a custom coin.
