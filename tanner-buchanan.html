Major Breakthrough Role Robby Keene IN Cobra Kai(TV series)

Cobra Kai is the continuation of the Karate Kid trilogy that was published in the 1980s. The first two seasons of the sequel series were produced from 2018 to 2019 by YouTube Red. Netflix bought the series in June 2020 and launched the third season on January 1, 2021.

It follows the lives of Ralph Macchio as Daniel LaRusso and William Zabka as Johnny Lawrence. And on October 24, 2017, actor Tanner Buchanan landed the main character as Robby Keene in Season One.

Apart from the show, Buchanan, 22, already made his breakthrough by playing the role Leo Kirkman on ABC political thriller drama Designated Survivor from 2016–2018. Also, he has worked in TV series like Game Shakers, Girl Meets World, Fuller House, The Fosters, and The Goldbergs. 

His films credits include Jake Squared, The Heyday of the Insensitive Bastards, Anything and Chance. 

Bio and Early Life

Tanner Buchanan was born on 8 December 1998 in Ohio, United States. He is the son of Steve Buchanan (father), and Marlona Buchanan (mother), a travelling businessman. Further, he has an older sister named Marrae Prowant.

He studied at Glandorf Elementary School. But later he shifted to Ottawa’s Center Stage Academy.
