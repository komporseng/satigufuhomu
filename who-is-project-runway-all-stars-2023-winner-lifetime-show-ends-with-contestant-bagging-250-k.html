Who is 'Project Runway All-Stars' 2023 winner? Lifetime show ends with contestant bagging $250K

May 2024 · 4 minute read

NEW YORK CITY, NEW YORK: 'Project Runway All-Stars' 2023 has finally come to an end and Bishme Cromartie took away the coveted title. The designer not only won Season 20 but also a lot of money along with it.

After being announced the winner, Bishme said, "I’ve dreamed of winning ‘Project Runway’ ever since I’d first seen Christian on his season."

He added, "To be here a second time and win, it honestly feels like the kid in me is just extremely proud and excited as hell. I owe this win to my sister, my biggest fan, the first person who didn’t judge me for doing something outside the norm."

What did 'Project Runway All-Stars' winner Bishme Cromartie take home?

'Project Runway All-Stars' Season 20 winner Bishme Cromartie bagged $250,000 and a mentorship with the Council of Fashion Designers of America. Well, this is not it, Bishme also gets a feature in Elle Magazine.

Nina Garcia and Eline Welterworth made their remarkable judgment on the finale night of Bravo's designing reality show.

Brittany Allen's collection was called "Pop-ggression." She described it as a progression of color and print that combines activewear with ready-to-wear. Lots of straps and buckles.

However, Bishme Cromartie's collection was called "Powering Through." And, Lawrence Basse's collection was called "One Night in Paris."

Why did 'Project Runway All-Stars' 2023 winner Bishme Cromartie break down?

Bishme broke down in the finale episode as he was grieving his sister Chimere Wall. He said, "I don't know if anyone could handle what I handled literally days before the show."

However, during the season, Bishme was seen putting his sister’s obituary at his workstation in the workroom. He did it to make himself feel at home and to remind himself what this whole journey meant to him.

Towards the final few minutes of the finale episode, Bishme's mother broke down witnessing her son achieve the greatest. 

Bishme's black "anxiety gown" that closed the show won judges' hearts. The voluminous skirt and the silhouette cut top were deemed one of the best designs of the runway moments. 

Billy Porter and Zac Posen join 'Project Runway All-Stars' 2023 on finale night

Billy Porter and Zac Posen joined the judges' panel on the finale night of 'Project Runway All-Stars' Season 20. Since Judge Brandon Maxwell was missing during the finale, Billy took his place. And, Zac became the guest judge of the hour.

Billy is a fashion designer while Zac is the CEO and founder of Alice + Olivia Stacy Bendet. This is Billy's second time as a guest judge on the Bravo show. However, he has been there for the first time in the finale episode.

Besides Billy and Zac, other judges who appeared throughout the season were Alicia Silverstone, creative director Carolina Herrera Wes Gordon, actor, producer, and Emmy-winning writer Lena Waithe, Law Roach, and Jennie Garth.

Who are the 'Project Runway All-Stars' 2023 finalists? 

The three contestants who made it to the finale round are Brittany Allen, Laurence Basse, and Bishme Cromartie. The three contestants will get $10,000 finalist money and an opportunity to shop anywhere in the fashion district.

Brittany chose to work with Fabio Costa, while Bishme went with Prajje Oscar Jean-Baptiste, and Laurence with Kara Saun.

However, Brittany got the first chance to pick her model in the finale episode. And, she went with MIMI TAO.

'Project Runway All-Stars' Season 20 is available to stream on Bravo.
