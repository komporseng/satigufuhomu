PD James's About

PD James was born on August 3, 1920 (age 93) in England. She is a Celebrity Novelist. An English crime writer, she is best known for her popular mystery series featuring the poetic detective, Adam Dalgliesh. Her non-serial works include Innocent Blood (1980), The Children of Men (1992), and Death Comes to Pemberley (2011). According to numerology, PD James's Life Path Number is 5.
