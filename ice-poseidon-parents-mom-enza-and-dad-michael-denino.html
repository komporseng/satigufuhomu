Who Are Ice Poseidon’s Parents?

Ice Poseidon, whose real name is Paul Denino, was born to his parents on 29 September 1994. As of now, there have been numerous occasions that Ice chose to bring his folks to the social media limelight.

Back in April 2017, when Ice posted on YouTube a video of him visiting his parents in Florida for the first time after 6 months, the “Poseidon Highlights” channel on YouTube reposted this and claimed in the description that Ice’s his do not seem to like him that much. They also mentioned Ice talking about referring to his relationship with his parents as a “strange” one.

Meet Enza Denino, Ice Poseidon’s Mom

Ice Poseidon’s mom’s real name is Vincenza A Abbate, but simply and after her married name she is also just simply called Enza Denimo.

Ice has often either just mentioned or other times called Enza in the middle of his streamings. He has been seen sometimes calling Enza at 2 a.m. to tell her some great news and other times just asking her his kind of weird questions.

Enza is the daughter of Anna Abbate, who passed away at the age of 87 in November 2021, and Paolo Abbate who passed at away 91 in March 2013. The late matriarch was born in Tunisia to Vito and Vita (Carrubba) Torregiano and was a member of St. Catherine Catholic Church. She enjoyed cooking, playing bingo, and sewing. She was preceded in death by her husband of 60 years, sons Vito, and Antonio Abbate, her brother Vincent Torregiano, and both her parents.

Speaking of Enza’s dad, he was born in Italy to Antonio and Vincenza (Garitta) Abbate. He was the owner and operator of a grocery store, and a restauranter and was a member of St. Catherine Catholic Church. Not to miss, he also served in the Italian Army Calvary during WWII and was a prisoner of war for 2 years. An Italian immigrant, he had come to the United States to live the American Dream.

Surprisingly, back in May 2018, Enza and her husband, together, appealed the trial court’s order denying their amended motion seeking sanctions against none other than Enza’s mom Anna Abbate.
