U-God Net Worth

U-God is an American rapper with a net worth of $5 million. He was born in Brooklyn, New York City in 1970 and is best known for being a member of the hip hop group Wu-Tang Clan. The group has sold more than 40 million albums worldwide and has had seven gold and platinum studio albums. U-God released his debut solo album Golden Arms Redemption in 1999, which reached #15 on the US R&B chart and #58 on the Billboard 200 chart. He has also released several other albums and collaborated with various artists, including members of Wu-Tang Clan and others such as Cypress Hill, Masta Killa, Cappadonna, Blondie, and Mobb Deep.
